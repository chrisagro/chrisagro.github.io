Niacinamide

Niacinamide, also known as vitamin B3, is a water-soluble vitamin that is found in many foods. In skincare products, niacinamide is known for its ability to improve the skin's moisture barrier, reduce inflammation, and improve the overall appearance of the skin. It can also help reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Some benefits of niacinamide include:


  • - Improves the skin's moisture barrier
  • - Reduces inflammation
  • - Improves the overall appearance of the skin
  • - Helps reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles

Niacinamide can be found in various skincare products such as moisturizers, serums, and toners. Always follow the product instructions and consult with a dermatologist before incorporating new skincare products into your routine.
